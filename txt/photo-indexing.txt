Photograph Indexing & Organisation

Transform thousands of scattered photos into a beautiful, searchable library

Over the years, digital photographs accumulate into thousands of files scattered across hard drives, USB sticks, old phones, and folders with names like "New Folder (2)". I'll take your entire collection and turn it into a beautifully organised, fully searchable library — so you can find any photo in seconds. Using the latest Artificial Intelligence tools, your library is automatically tagged with locations, faces, and events, making it easy to search for that holiday in 2015, find every photo of a loved one, or relive a cherished memory at a moment's notice.

What's Included:

Collection of photos from computers, laptops, phones, USB drives and memory cards

Removal of exact duplicates to reclaim storage space

Automatic organisation by date, event and location

AI-powered face recognition to group photos by person

AI location tagging — find photos by place even without GPS data

Smart scene detection: beaches, birthdays, landscapes, pets and more

Easy-to-use library software set up on your computer

Keyword and full-text search across your entire collection

Backup strategy to keep your memories safe going forward

Handover walkthrough so you feel confident using your new library
