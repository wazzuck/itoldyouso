POS & Payment Systems

Get your payment setup working reliably from day one

Whether you're a small business, market trader, or sole trader, getting your payment system set up correctly is critical. I can help you choose, install, and configure point-of-sale (POS) and card payment systems that work seamlessly with your business. From contactless card readers to full till systems integrated with UK banks and payment providers, I'll make sure everything connects properly and reliably.

Systems & Providers I Work With:

  • SumUp – card reader setup and account configuration
  • Square – POS hardware, software, and dashboard setup
  • iZettle (by PayPal) – reader pairing and app configuration
  • Zettle by PayPal – full till and inventory setup
  • Stripe Terminal – developer and retail POS integration
  • Barclaycard Anywhere – pairing and troubleshooting
  • Lloyds Cardnet – terminal setup and network configuration
  • Worldpay – terminal installation and connectivity
  • Clover POS – full system setup and staff training
  • Lightspeed & Shopify POS – retail and hospitality setups

What I Can Help With:

  • Card reader and terminal setup
  • WiFi and network configuration for payment devices
  • Integration with accounting software (Xero, QuickBooks, Sage)
  • Receipt printer and cash drawer setup
  • Inventory and product catalogue configuration
  • Staff accounts and permissions
  • Troubleshooting connectivity and payment failures
  • Migrating from one payment provider to another
